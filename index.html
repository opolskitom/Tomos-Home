about

Hello! My name is Tomo (...or Thomas formally). I am a recent graduate from the University of Michigan, Dearborn with a Bachelor's in Computer Science. A lot of my time spent in college was dealing with personal struggles and since then, I have done as much as I can to both better myself for the future, and achieve my goal of becoming a software developer in this extrememly competitive industry. What I lack in experience, I guarentee to make up for in effort and dedication, and I hope to be viewed as a competitive candidate for vital positions within a company that allows me to learn, grow, and create lasting relations with others.

My skills and technologies range from front-end design in HTML, CSS, XML in Android Studio, pyQt, and Unity, to experience in backend languages such as C++, Python, JavaScript, SQL, and Java. I am knowledgeable with Agile methodologies such as Scrum, and am familiar with project management tools such as Git and GitHub. I am also working to become fluent in Japanese, having studied abroad to further learn the language and gain invaluable cross-cultural experiences. Currently, I am primarily searching for entry-level software, mobile, and web application developer positions to get my feet off the ground and really start contributing to large-scale, team-based projects.
